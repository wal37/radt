Technical Breakdown

Four systems working in one production stack.

Serious quality comes from an orchestrated pipeline rather than one model call. Ekialo is positioned around the parts that make brand outputs believable and scalable.

1

Garment Understanding Engine

Transforms clothing uploads into usable structure for downstream rendering.

  • Texture and material extraction
  • Fit and silhouette understanding
  • Part segmentation across collars, sleeves, trims, and seams
2

AI Avatar and Model Generator

Produces controllable human subjects built for campaign and commerce outputs.

  • Gender, skin tone, hair, and body profile controls
  • Pose, expression, and energy direction
  • Preset libraries or fully generative profiles
3

Virtual Try-On System

The realism layer that determines whether the output feels premium or fake.

  • Drape, fold, and stretch behavior
  • Occlusion and body-cloth interaction
  • Shadow, lighting, and fit fidelity
4

Image and Video Generation Pipeline

Delivers the final assets in formats brands can actually use.

  • Studio stills and lifestyle scenes
  • Walkthrough, runway, and short-form motion output
  • Batch rendering for large product catalogs
